Open Development Cambodia (ODC) is an open data website, the first of its kind in Southeast Asia. The open data movement is based on the simple premise that data collected for public interest should be publicly available without restrictions. Information or data in the public domain should be freely available to everyone to use and republish as they wish. Open Development Cambodia does not promote any particular perspective, agenda or bias other than to provide objective information about Cambodia and its development. ODC is looking for candidates with a strong motivation and work experiences for Information Technology/Website Manager positions as below:

Information Technology/Website Manager

Term of Reference

Position : Information Technology/Website Manager
Reports to : Information Technology/Website Director and ODC Executive Director/Editor-in Chief
Coordinates with : Editor-Research team, Data Researcher and GIS team, Regional IT Manager and Other staffs

Primary Responsibilities

The Information Technology/Website Manager works with the ODC team in developing online information resources (www.opendevcam.net) for Cambodia's development community and other audiences. The Information Technology/Website Manager takes the lead role in developing and maintaining the ODC web platform and controlling the networking system.

Main Responsibilities:

  • Develop, manage and maintain ODC platform and network system to ensure well performance and security.
  • Provide assistance and backstopping for other Open Development sites when the Information Technology/Website Director and Regional IT Manager are not available.
  • Support the Executive Director in the development and implementation of ODC’s strategic plans and policies.
  • Responsible for the development and execution of ODC’s technical strategy in partnerships with regional and other national partners.
  • Provide leadership, guidance, and direction to the IT Officer, Web Developers, Mappers and other technical staffs, setting an effective agenda and ensuring performance goals are set and then met.
  • Provide overall technical support to the Data Researcher and GIS team and Editor-Researcher team, coding to upload and present data in various formats of open data, build mapping functions, correct errors and enhance the functionality of the site for user-friendliness.
  • Develop project plans, timelines, project documentation and promotional materials
  • Serve as a mentor, coach and guide to ODC staffs.

Detailed Duties and Tasks
ODC IT Management and Web Platform Management

  • Ensure maintenance and optimization of the ODC in-house server for both back-end and front-end efficiency and security; identify problems and work with technical team to ensure regular in-house server updates are made and maintenance and timely solutions to problems are carried out.
  • Advise and support ODC staffs in uploading spatial and non-spatial data to the database and website in a timely way.
  • Manage technical aspects of the platform (WordPress, CKAN and GeoServer), including guiding, directing and monitoring staff in coding and data entry.
  • Design and develop infographics, data visualization, maps, and website template in consultation with the ODC team.
  • Maintain social media accounts, identify and resolve any technical problems associated with them.
  • Track and monitor website and social media analytics.
  • Train, guide and advice ODC staffs on using WordPress, CKAN, GeoServer and other applications as needed, and ensure that they implement following the OD standard.
  • In consultation with Information Technology/Website Director or Regional IT Manager, identify and recommend software solutions to address particular design, mapping or data visualization issues and guide staff in their applications.
  • Design ODC promotional and presentation materials, as requested, in keeping with OD standards.
  • Identify errors and new IT needs and propose solutions to Information Technology/Website Director, Regional IT manager and other IT managers; participate in discussion and consultation to decide and agree on any new features, customizations, or revisions.
  • With the supervision of the Information Technology/Website Director, Regional IT Manager and in collaboration with other team members, assist in design new templates, customize themes, develop functions, build plug-ins, and code revisions to correct errors.
  • Orient staff to security policies and protocols and ensure their implementation; this may include periodic audits of staff computers.
  • Track and monitor internal network and server and ensure that network system is secured and backup properly.
  • In consultation with the Information Technology/Website Director and Regional IT Manager, configure, control, and maintain the local network systems for security and online data is centralized.
  • Identify short and long-term technical problems and coordinate with other staff to determine ongoing technical needs.
  • In consultation with the Information Technology/Website Director and Regional IT Manager and relevant staffs, research and make recommendations on IT equipment procurement.
  • Monitor and report on progress toward project goals.
  • Research, develop, create project documentation and guidelines, and maintain the site changelog.

ODC Operations and Strategy

  • Participate in strategic development of the ODC and ODMekong platform overall.
  • Perform other tasks as needed or assigned by ODC Executive Director/Editor-in-Chief.
